Lithium Iron

Lithium Iron Phosphate (LiFePO₄) batteries are a subtype of lithium-ion batteries known for exceptional thermal stability, long cycle life, and safety. While they offer lower energy density than other lithium chemistries, they are highly durable, cost-effective, and ideal for applications that demand reliability over compact size.

Pros Cons
Long cycle life Slightly larger and heavier
Environmentally friendly Lower energy density
Non-toxic Less suitable fo high-voltage
