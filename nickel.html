Nickel-Metal Hydride

Nickel-Metal Hydride (NiMH) batteries are rechargeable batteries known for their durability, safety, and moderate energy density. While they are heavier than lithium-ion batteries, NiMH batteries offer a longer lifespan and better tolerance to overcharging, making them a reliable choice for hybrid vehicles and consumer electronics.

Pros Cons
More environmentally friendly Lower energy density
Safer and more stable Higher self-discharge rate
Long cycle life Heavier and bulkier
